<p style='text-align: justify;'>
Kasmita Singha is a fourth-year BS-MS student at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Bhopal, India, where she conducts research in the Laboratory of Molecular and Macromolecular Materials under the supervision of Prof. Aasheesh Srivastava. 
Her research interests encompass strain chemistry, synthetic organic chemistry, and polymer chemistry. 
Between May and July 2026, she is visiting the research group of Dr. André K. Eckhardt at Ruhr University Bochum through the RESOLV Undergraduate Exchange Program.
