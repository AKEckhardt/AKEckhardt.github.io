<p style='text-align: justify;'>
Barbara Keresztes is a PhD student in the Laboratory of Molecular Spectroscopy at Eötvös Loránd University in Budapest, Hungary, under the supervision of Dr. György Tarczay. Her research focuses on astrochemistry; in her work, she performs laboratory experiments and supports them with quantum chemical calculations. Between March and May of 2026 she is visiting the reserch group of Dr. André K. Eckhardt at Ruhr Universität Bochum with the support of RESOLV Student Challenge "Your Step into Solvation Science".
