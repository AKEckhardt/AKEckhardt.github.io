<p style='text-align: justify;'>
Anita studied at Eötvös Loránd University (ELTE), where she obtained a BSc degree in Chemistry in 2018 and an MSc degree in Chemistry in 2020. 
She then continued her studies at the Hevesy György PhD School of Chemistry at ELTE. Her diploma research projects were carried out in the Laboratory of Molecular Spectroscopy under the supervision of <a href="https://msl.chem.elte.hu/tarczay/index.html">Prof. György Tarczay</a>. In her PhD work, she focused on astrochemically relevant reactions, using the <i>para</i>-H<sub>2</sub> matrix isolation technique and modelling reactions in astrophysical analog ices.
